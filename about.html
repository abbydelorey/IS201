I love ... My Job!

I am lucky to have multiple amazing jobs on campus, but my favorite by far is being head teaching assistant for Intro to Computer Science (CS111)! I get the opportunity to lead recitation for two sections of this class, meaning I get to teach 100 awesome people two times a week! I love the connections I've made with my fellow TAs and especially my students. I find it really rewarding to seeing how successful my students become. Even though I am not pursuing a computer science degree anymore, this job and the people I get to work with will always have a large impact on me!
